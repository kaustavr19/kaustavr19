### 🧑‍💻 About Me

> *"I love creating innovative and truly impactful solutions by designing better interfaces and crafting meaningful user experiences."*

I'm a multi-disciplinary **Product (UI/UX) Designer** with **3.5+ years** of experience across startups and enterprise environments. My work sits at the intersection of **UX strategy, interaction design, and behavioral science** — currently focused on agentic AI systems and tools for knowledge work.

Before Fractal, I led design at **Eugenie.ai** during the Google for Startups Accelerator, where the **Sustainability module I designed was featured on the Google I/O 2024 stage** — one of my proudest achievements. I'm also a passionate **design mentor**, content creator, and serial hackathon competitor.
